HMS, or Hospital Management System, is a comprehensive application designed to handle a wide range of tasks related to hospital operations. This web-based app offers a multitude of functionalities, including online appointment booking, digital access to patient reports, and streamlined tracking of patients by doctors. With its all-encompassing capabilities, HMS provides a digital solution for various hospital management aspects. Patients benefit from the convenience of accessing their reports digitally, while doctors can efficiently manage patient records.
